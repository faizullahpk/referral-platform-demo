Frequently Asked Questions
How long does deposit approval take?
Deposits are manually verified by our team within 2–24 hours. You will receive a notification once your plan is activated.
When do I receive my daily profit?
Daily profits are automatically credited to your balance every time you open your dashboard. If you haven't logged in for a few days, all missed profits will be credited at once.
How does the referral commission work?
You earn 2% of every deposit made by users who signed up with your referral code. The commission is credited automatically to your balance when the admin approves their payment.
Minimum and maximum withdrawal amounts?
Minimum withdrawal is Rs 500. There is no maximum limit. Withdrawals are processed within 2–24 hours via JazzCash.
What if my withdrawal shows approved but I didn't receive money?
Check the Transaction ID shown in your withdrawal history. You can verify this ID directly in your JazzCash app. If the ID is valid but money hasn't arrived, contact support on WhatsApp immediately.
How do I unlock higher investment packages?
You must first complete the Rs 500 Starter package. Once approved, all Field A, B, and C packages will be unlocked for you automatically.
